<div class="cusInstText1" style="height: 91%; width: 100%; overflow: auto;"><p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><u><b>Paper Specific Instructions </b></u></p> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol>  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm">The examination is of <i><b>3 hours</b></i> duration. There are a total of <i><b>100 questions</b></i> carrying a total of <i><b>300 marks</b></i>. The entire paper is divided into three sections, <b>A</b>, <b>B</b> and <b>C</b>. All sections are compulsory. Questions in each section are of different types.</p>  </li> </ol> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol start="2">  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><b>Section A </b><b>(60 Marks)</b> contains a total of 20 <b>Numerical Answer Type (NAT)</b> questions. For these NAT type questions, the answer is a real number that needs to be entered using the virtual keyboard on the monitor. No choices will be shown for these questions.There is NEGATIVE marking for this section. Each <i>correct answer</i> will be awarded <i>3 marks</i> and each <i>wrong answer</i> will receive <i>−1 (minus 1)</i> mark. Questions <i>not attempted</i> will result in <i>zero marks</i>. Questions 1-20 belong to this section.</p>  </li> </ol> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol start="3">  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><b>Section B </b><b>(90 Marks)</b> contains a total of 30 <b>Multiple Select Questions (MSQ)</b>. Each MSQ type question may have <b>one or more than one correct choice(s) </b>that are correct out of the four given choices.The candidate gets 3 marks <strong>only if</strong> ALL the correct answers and no wrong answers are selected. There is NO NEGATIVE or PARTIAL marking for this section. Questions <i>not attempted</i> will be given <i>zero marks</i>. Questions 21-50 belong to this section.</p>  </li> </ol> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol start="4">  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><b>Section C </b><b>(150 Marks)</b> contains a total of 50 <b>Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)</b>. Each MCQ has four choices out of which <b>only one </b>is the correct answer.There is NEGATIVE marking for this section. Each <i>correct answer</i> will be awarded <i>3 marks</i> and each <i>wrong answer</i> will receive <i>−1 (minus 1) </i>marks. Questions <i>not attempted </i>will be given <i>zero marks</i>. Questions 51-100 belong to this section.</p>  </li> </ol> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol start="5">  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm">Calculators, charts, graph sheets, tables, cellular phone or other electronic gadgets are <b>NOT</b> allowed in the examination hall.</p>  </li> </ol> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <ol start="6">  <li>  <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm">A Scribble Pad will be provided for rough work.</p>  </li> </ol> <p style="margin-left: 1.27cm; margin-bottom: 0cm"> </p> <p class="western" style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><span style="background-color:#ffff00;">NOTE: The above instructions are of UCEED 2015, The instructions for UCEED 2017 will be different and must be read at the time of UCEED 2017 exam.
